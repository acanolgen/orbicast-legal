Frequently asked

How do I create a flyover?
Open the Studio tab, search for a place or move the map to your spot, then tap “Render Drone View.” Your cinematic flyover appears in moments and is saved under “My Renders.”

What is Orbicast Pro?
A subscription that unlocks unlimited renders and unlimited flight time. Weekly, monthly and yearly plans are available. Subscriptions are charged to your Apple ID and auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the period. Manage or cancel anytime in your iPhone Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.

How do I restore a purchase?
On the paywall, tap “Restore.”

Do I need a real drone?
No. Orbicast creates the flyover for you from real 3D map data — no drone or skills required.
